Welcome to the Appblocks Refund Rules

This Refund Policy contains the terms and conditions under which refunds may be provided to Customers for Author Content and/or Author Services purchased on or through the Platform. By making a purchase on the Marketplace or the Platform, You agree to be bound by this Refund Policy. If You are a Customer, this Refund Policy is a part of the Customer Terms and Conditions.

If You are an Author, this Refund Policy is a part of the Author Terms and Conditions. Capitalized terms used but not defined in this Refund Policy, have the meaning assigned to them in the applicable terms and conditions agreement.

Since the products sold by Authors on our Platform are digital goods, they cannot be “returned.” Therefore, we have designed our refund policy with this in mind to ensure that you are entitled to a fair refund if necessary.

1. General Refund Conditions

All sales of Author Content and/or Author Services on the Platform are considered final, and payment obligations are non-cancellable. Amounts paid for Purchasable Content and/or Services are generally non-refundable, as specified in Section 3.5 of the Customer Terms and Conditions. In case you encounter a technical problem with an item you purchased from the “Blocks Store” on Appblocks, we suggest that you contact the Author of the item for assistance. Often they’ll be able to help to troubleshoot your problem.

2. Refund Request Process

To request a refund, Customers must contact the Author directly, providing a clear explanation of the reason for the refund request, along with any supporting documentation, such as proof of purchase, within the specified refund timeframe.

3. Refund Timeframe

Customers must submit their refund request within 14 days of: (i) the original purchase date; or (ii) the date on which the reason for requesting a refund first occurs* . Refund requests submitted beyond this timeframe may not be eligible for a refund.

* Alternative language that allows refunds in cases where the breach happens at a later date:

within 14 days of: (i) the original purchase date; or (ii) the date on which the reason for requesting a refund first occurs.

4. Eligibility for Refunds

  1. Refunds may be provided to Customers only in the event of:
    1. Breach of Author’s obligations related to the purchased Content or Services, such as the Content being “not as described” or the item not working the way that it should, a security vulnerability in the Content that is not fixed by the Author, or if support is required under the Agreement but not provided to Customer;
    2. if updates are promised but the Content is removed;
    3. otherwise explicitly agreed upon between the Author and Customer; and/or
    4. if a refund is required by Applicable Law.
  2. In the event of refusal by Author to provide a refund or inability to contact Author, Company may, at its sole discretion, provide refunds on behalf of the Author and withhold or recover the amount of such refunds from the Author.
  3. Content is considered “not as described” if it substantially differs from the item description or preview.

5. Dispute Resolution

  1. Disputes related to Author Content and/or Author Services, including but not limited to disputes related to refunds, licenses provided, revocation of licenses by the Author, and third-party intellectual property rights, must be resolved between the Author and Customer directly, as stated in Sections 3.3.4 of the Customer Terms and Conditions, and Section 3.3.7 of Author Terms and Conditions.
  2. While the Company is not a party to any such dispute, it reserves the right, but not the obligation, to act on behalf of the Author and/or Customer in order to settle any dispute between them, at its sole discretion. We may request that You provide evidence or supporting documents. Please note that any refund issued by the Company is entirely at our sole discretion. We will carefully consider all relevant information, and You agree to accept Our decision as final and binding.

6. Effects of Refunds

  1. In the event of a refund, the Company may, at its sole discretion, reduce amounts due to the Author, withhold the Author’s future earnings, or request the Author to return any amounts already paid, as specified in Section 3.5 of the Author Terms and Conditions.
  2. The Company may also withhold future payments to the Author to recover any processing fees lost (or paid) as a result of such refunds or unsuccessful payments.

7. Refund Method

If a refund is granted, it will be processed using the original payment method used by the Customer during the purchase, unless otherwise agreed upon by both the Author and Customer.

8. Exceptions and Exclusions

  1. Refunds may be denied in cases where the Customer:
    1. Has misused the Content or Author Services;
    2. Violated the Customer Terms and Conditions, applicable Content License(s), this Refund Policy, or the applicable Order Form(s); and/or
    3. Engaged in unauthorized access or distribution of Content or Author Services;
  2. In addition to the above, refunds are not applicable for the following situations:
    1. Customer does not want the Content or Author Service(s) after purchasing it, regardless of download and usage;
    2. The Content or Author Service did not meet Customer’s expectations or the Customer considers the item to be of low quality;
    3. Customer changed its mind about purchasing the Content or Author Service(s);
    4. Customer bought a Content or Author Service by mistake;
    5. Customer does not have sufficient expertise to use the Content;
    6. Customer claims that it is entitled to a refund but does not provide sufficient information regarding the reason for such being entitled to a refund, or does not provide evidence or supporting documents; and/or
    7. The Author that previously provided the Content or Author Service is no longer active on the Platform or Marketplace.

9. Changes to Services

In the event that the purchased Content or Author Services undergo significant changes, rendering them unusable or materially different from the original description, Customers may be eligible for a refund, subject to the Author’s discretion and applicable refund conditions.

10. Modifications

The Company reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time, at its sole discretion. Changes to the Refund Policy will be effective immediately upon posting on the Platform. Your continued use of the Platform following the posting of an updated Refund Policy indicates Your acceptance and agreement to be bound by the modified Refund Policy.

11. Customer Support

  1. Customers who have questions, concerns, or require assistance related to refunds or the Refund Policy can contact the Author directly for support, using the provided contact information.
  2. Customers are unable to resolve their refund-related issues directly with the Author, they may contact the Platform's support team for assistance, although the Platform is not obligated to intervene in disputes between Authors and Customers.
